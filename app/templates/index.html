about dublin bike

DublinBike is a smart and sustainable bike-sharing platform designed to make moving around Dublin faster, easier, and greener.

Launched in 2025 as part of a student-led innovation project, the platform combines real-time station data, live weather integration, and AI-powered predictions to help users plan their rides with confidence. With an interactive city map, clean interface, and mobile-friendly design, DublinBike is built for both locals and visitors. Whether you're commuting to work, heading to class, or just exploring the city, DublinBike helps you ride smarter—anytime, anywhere.
